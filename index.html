Why Go Vanilla?

There's probably no description suitable to vinyl records as good as saying old but gold. It is a classic piece for a reason, being perhaps the only format able to almost physically encapsulate music of any genre. Whether you're trying out your first record or are in it for a big run, there are pretty good reasons why you should go vanilla.


Our thorough process of getting your music into the records, along with top-of-the-line materials and machinery, ensure only the best quality for you and your listeners.

Our new innovative way of producing vinyl records make way for faster turnaround times. No need to wait for almost half a year to get your music project done!

Your experience and journey is important to us. This is why we work hard behind curtains to make sure you have smooth, stress-free transactions.

As passionate music lovers, we know and understand all the blood, tears, and sweat music creators spend for their masterpiece. We respect that dedication by committing impeccable service as well to our clientele. We are here for you, and you can count on us!
